Work Plan

2026 Timeline

Monthly follow-up by the CDA and ACA Mobility Team.

End of July 2026

Build Kit Delivery

All teams receive the same homologated kit. Construction of electric cars begins at each school.

August — October 2026

Monthly Follow-up + Audiovisual Content

The CDA and Mobility Team conduct monthly follow-ups. Authorized audiovisual material is requested to publish progress on social media.

October — November 2026

Outreach & Team Spotlights

Through ACA social media, teams are introduced, their stories shared, and the project's impact on each educational community highlighted.

November 6, 7 & 8, 2026

Competition in Junín, Buenos Aires

150 teams compete (10 members each, 2 teachers). Women's Grand Sprint. Press events at the local ACA facility.

Mandatory: female driver Women's Grand Sprint Press Events
